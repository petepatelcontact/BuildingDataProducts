Background

This application allows you to determine distance available for driving based on remaining fuel in tank. Typically we say we have used 1/2 or 1/4 of tank and want to know how far we can go with remaining tank. Input the Fuel Capacity of the car and percent of gas tank used and the application will calculate distance car can travel on the remaining gas in tank.

The user will get to know the fuel efficiency of their car by getting:

  • Fuel Capacity of their Car
  • MPG that the Car provides
  • Monitor their Gas Gauage proactively

Application Parameters

Following are the input parameters that the user provides

  • Tank Size in gallons (range of 18 to 40 is allowed)
  • Miles per gallon (range of 8mpg to 50mpg allowed)
  • Percent of tank used

Calculations

There are two calculation performed in the application

  • Based on percent of gas used determine number of gallons remaining
  • Based on the three inputs, tank size, mpg and percent tank used determine remaining miles

Example

For example, based on following parameters:

  • Tank Size = 18
  • Mpg = 23
  • Tank Used (%) = 50
tankSize <- 18; mpg <- 23; tankUsed <- 50
remainingGallons <- (tankSize * (1-(tankUsed/100)))
remainingDistance <- (tankSize * (1-(tankUsed/100))) * mpg
remainingGallons

## [1] 9

remainingDistance
